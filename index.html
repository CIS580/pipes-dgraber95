Play Pipes

Instructions

1. Your goal is to connect the starting pipe (clear) to the ending pipe (red) without letting water escape the pipes

2. Collect points by making the path as long as possible (10 points per pipe)

3. Use mouse to move pipes and place them by left clicking

4. Rotate already placed pipes by hovering the mouse over them and right clicking

5. Rotate your next pipe by right clicking in a cell that doesn't already contain a pipe

6. Press 'P' to pause and unpause game

7. Press and hold the spacebar to speed the flow of water
